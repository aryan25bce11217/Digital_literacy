This project belongs to a digital literacy portfolio developed as a part of CSE0001 course at

digital skills required for academic growth and professional growth.  
As a student of digital literacy course I explored on different areas of digitial literacy and 
important of this course for students such as : 
° creating a digital content for students for their professional growth.

° using  a coding platforms and collaboration for students.  
° To practice proffesional communication

All tasks which is mentioned above are organised in  github repository to demonstrate

##  Task Summary

###  Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
Creating an infographic using canva to explain digital literacy concepts such as safe internet 
practices, useful tools, and professional online behavior for students for their proffesional 
growth. 

---

###  Task 2: Digital Portfolio
Building a professional profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to establish 
a digital identity and showcase skills which is helpful for students.  

---

###  Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Solving  a beginner-level problem on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz to 
understand collaboration and data collection tools which is helpful for students.  


---

###  Task 4: Email Etiquette & Social Media Awareness
Drafting a professional emails and create a checklist of responsible social media practices 
for students. 

---

###  Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
Preparing a case study on UPI fraud and created a prevention checklist with safety tips and 
reporting methods.
